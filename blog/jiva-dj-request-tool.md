# Jiva: Solving DJ Song Request Chaos

If you've ever DJed an event, you know the drill: guests constantly come up to ask for songs, pulling you out of your flow. You have to stop mixing, search for the track, download it, add it to your library, all while trying to keep the vibe going. It's a headache that kills the energy of a set.

Jiva fixes this. It's a simple tool that lets guests request songs without you lifting a finger. Here's how it works:

Guests scan a QR code you display (no app download needed), enter the song name and artist. Jiva automatically searches YouTube for the official music video, filters out low-quality or wrong tracks, downloads the best available audio, converts it to MP3 with embedded metadata and thumbnails, and drops it straight into your Mixxx library folder. Mixxx picks it up automatically, so it's ready to play in seconds.

It also handles the small stuff that matters: it won't download the same song twice, supports creating event playlists to bulk-download tracks ahead of time, and runs quietly in the background so you can focus on DJing. No more interruptions, no more manual work, just seamless song requests that keep the party going.
